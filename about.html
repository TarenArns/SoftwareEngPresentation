About our Project

Vision Statement

The Study Buddy quiz tool will provide students with practice for tests and examinations with flashcard like questions. Users will be able to create and modify flashcards and design their own practice questions. It will provide users a way to run quizzes based on these flashcards, which will show them feedback at the end.

Major Functionality

Users can create accounts to share the app with others on the same device.

Flashcards can be created for each account, allowing users to study as many questions as they can come up with.

Don’t worry if you make a mistake, you can edit the flashcards with a simple click of a button!

Running a quiz will provide you with a choice of how many flash cards to display.

At the end of each quiz, you will receive a score of how well you did.

Flashcards also track success rate, so you can see how you’re performing with each question and focus your studying where its needed most

Intended Users

Useful for students who want to get an upper hand on their peers by studying with a more structured approach to studying.

Teachers or Instructors wanting to engage with students through new interactive technology-based ways of studying over traditional pen and paper.

Professional workers looking to improve their knowledge and skills for that upcoming performance review and get that raise they deserve.
